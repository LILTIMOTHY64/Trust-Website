Who We Are

Parisu Charitable and Educational Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of young women in need. Founded on the principles of compassion, equality, and education, we strive to create a world where every girl has the opportunity to pursue her dreams, regardless of her circumstances.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower female students facing various challenges such as financial constraints, societal pressures, or personal hardships. Through our programs and initiatives, we aim to:

  • Provide financial assistance for education-related expenses
  • Offer mentorship and guidance to navigate difficult situations
  • Create a supportive community where women can share their stories and inspire each other
  • Advocate for gender equality and women's rights in education and beyond

How You Can Help

Together, we can make a difference. Whether you're a concerned individual, a corporate partner, or a philanthropic organization, there are many ways you can support our cause:

  • Donate: Your generous contributions help us fund scholarships, mentorship programs, and other essential services.
  • Volunteer: Join us in our efforts to mentor and support young women in need. Your time and expertise can make a significant impact.
  • Spread the Word: Help us raise awareness about our mission and reach more students who could benefit from our support. Share our website and social media channels with your network.

Submit a Request

If you or someone you know is a female student facing difficulties and in need of assistance, we're here to help. Submit a request through our website, and our team will review your application with care and confidentiality.
